Our services

We simplify your financial management. Below you can see our most popular services.

Bookkeeping

We continuously record all business transactions in your company and perform reconciliations. If anything is missing, we will contact you.

Payroll Management

We assist you with employees and ensure that accurate salaries are paid and reported.

Tax Filing

With ongoing accounting, we ensure that VAT and employer declarations are submitted on time to the tax authorities.

Reporting

After each accounting period, we send you a financial report and receipts from submitted declarations.

Year-end Closing

At the end of the fiscal year, we can start the year-end closing process and submit income tax returns.

Consulting

We help you run your business, identifying improvements and optimizing taxes.

Accounting

We manage all your company's administrative work, including bookkeeping, payroll management, tax returns, and financial statements. You can feel safe knowing that all business transactions are continuously recorded and reconciliations are carefully performed. We ensure that VAT and employer declarations are submitted on time to the Tax Agency and that year-end closings and income tax returns are handled correctly.

Digitization

We use the latest technology to manage your bookkeeping digitally, no matter where you are. All documents can be uploaded digitally, and many can be automatically sent to the accounting software we work with. We can also create payment files that you can easily import into your corporate bank.

Consulting

We help you run your business and offer consulting. Our goal is to simplify your administration and find opportunities for improvements. We optimize taxes based on your specific needs and are always available to answer your questions.

Tailored solutions

Every company is unique, which is why we offer tailored solutions that fit your specific needs. Whether you need help with bookkeeping, payroll management, tax filing or consulting, we tailor our services to meet your requirements and simplify your day-to-day business operations.
