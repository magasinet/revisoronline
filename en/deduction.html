Deduction of costs for businesses in Sweden

Below are links to expenses a company can have. Read more about each cost and see if you can make deductions and get VAT back. Remember that the rules may be different in some cases. Contact us if you want to be sure about your specific case.

  • Car (purchase/leasing of car, operating costs)
  • Electronics (computer, mobile, printer)
  • Financial costs (bank fee, bank loan, interests)
  • Materials (consumables, goods, equipment)
  • Rent costs (residence, rent, electricity, parking)
  • Representation (food, drink, and parties)
  • Staff costs (salaries, benefits, pension)
  • Other costs (accounting, IT services, legal fees, advertising)
