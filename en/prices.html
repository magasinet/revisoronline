FAQ

Here you can read more about frequently asked questions and answers

What counts as a voucher?

All transactions on the company's bank account (purchases, sales, and transfers) count as vouchers. This also includes employee expenses, VAT reporting, and transactions on your tax account. If you have a cash register, each daily cash report counts as a voucher. Do you bookkeep with accrual method, customer receivables and supplier invoices should be recorded twice, which counts as two vouchers per invoice.

What should I do if none of the price packages suit my needs?

Contact us so we can tailor a package for you. Sometimes, a company may have high revenue with few transactions, in which case we can offer a better price in these situations.

How are the monthly packages priced if the number of vouchers varies greatly between different months?

We can calculate an average for the entire year to provide you with a fair price that reflects your actual needs.

What is the notice period?

We have no notice periods for our services. If you wish to cancel a package, you can contact us. Upon cancellation, we will complete the accounting for the remainder of the month and submit the final tax declaration.

What happens if I exceed the number of vouchers included in my package?

If the exceedance is occasional with few vouchers, you can continue with the same package. If you consistently exceed the number of transactions, we will contact you to discuss any necessary price adjustments.

Are there any additional fees?

If you want access to an invoicing program, there is an additional fee of 139 SEK/month. If you require an active bank connection, banks typically charge a monthly fee. Sending and receiving e-invoices costs 3 kr per invoice.
